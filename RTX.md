**RTX GRAPHICs**
> A lightweight lighting manipulator that forces "RTX" style graphics on any Roblox game without lagging your PC.

🎛️ Live Sliders: Adjust bloom, sun rays, saturation, and PBR reflections on the fly.
🌧️ Glossy World: One-click toggle to make the whole map look wet and reflective.
🎨 Presets: Instantly switch between RTX Ultra, Vibrant Pop, or a gritty Cinematic look.
🛡️ Clean Unhook: Hitting the 'X' completely restores the game's original lighting and deletes the GUI.
